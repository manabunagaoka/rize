## VERIFIED CANDIDATES

### 1. **Zillow Group (Z, ZG)**
- **Founder**: Rich Barton (Harvard '89)
- **Year Founded**: 2006
- **Market Cap**: ~$10B
- **Category**: Consumer/Real Estate
- **Elevator Pitch**: "Empower people with data and tools to make smarter decisions about homes"
- **Fun Fact**: Rich also founded Expedia. Zillow uses "Zestimate" algorithm that values 100M+ homes.
- **Harvard Connection**: VERIFIED - Rich Barton graduated Harvard 1989

### 2. **HubSpot (HUBS)**
- **Founders**: Brian Halligan & Dharmesh Shah
- **Year Founded**: 2006
- **Market Cap**: ~$25B
- **Category**: Enterprise/B2B (Marketing Software)
- **Elevator Pitch**: "Inbound marketing platform that helps businesses attract customers"
- **Fun Fact**: The term "inbound marketing" was coined by HubSpot founder Brian Halligan
- **Harvard Connection**: Brian Halligan - Harvard MBA '05. Dharmesh Shah - MIT undergrad, but met Brian at MIT Sloan (need to verify)
- **Status**: STRONG CANDIDATE
